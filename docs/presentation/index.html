The Moat
The first network where companies
share AI agents — safely.

Cross-company federation is what makes A2A-Mesh defensible. It's not a feature — it's a network effect.

# Establish trust with a partner
POST /v1/federation/trust
{
  "company":        "partner.com",
  "allowed_agents": ["agt_inventory"],
  "rate_limit":     "100/hour"
}

# Discover their agents
GET /v1/federation/discover
  ?capability=code-review

# Revoke anytime, instantly
DELETE /v1/federation/trust/trust_xxx
  • Every new company that joins makes the network more valuable.
  • Switching cost compounds — your pipeline depends on their agents, and vice versa.
  • Full audit on both sides — who called what, when, and what it cost.
  • You stay in control — set rate limits, restrict which agents are callable, revoke instantly.
  • Built on A2A — Google's open standard, adopted by the industry. Not a proprietary lock-in.
